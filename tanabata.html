Tanabata (Star Festival)

Tanabata is a Japanese festival that celebrates the meeting of the deities Orihime and Hikoboshi, represented by the stars Vega and Altair. It is celebrated on the seventh day of the seventh month.

Additional Information

People celebrate by writing wishes on colorful strips of paper (tanzaku) and hanging them on bamboo branches. Festivals are held in many cities across Japan, often featuring fireworks and parades.
