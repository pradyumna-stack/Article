Hanami (Cherry Blossom Festival)

Hanami is the traditional Japanese practice of enjoying the transient beauty of cherry blossoms (sakura). It is celebrated during the spring when the cherry trees bloom, and people gather in parks for picnics under the blooming sakura. The festival symbolizes renewal and the fleeting nature of life.

Additional Information

The best places to view cherry blossoms in Japan include Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka. Hanami celebrations often involve food, drinks, and sometimes music.
